Privacy Policy

Offline TTS App

Last updated: September 15, 2025

📌 General Information

This Offline TTS app is developed individually to help users convert text into speech without an internet connection.

🔐 Information Collected

  • This app does not collect personal data such as name, email, or location.
  • All text-to-speech processing is done locally on your device.

📢 Third-Party Ads

This app displays ads from third-party providers (such as Google AdMob). These third parties may collect non-personal data such as device ID or device type to serve more relevant ads.

For more information about how Google handles data in its advertising products, visit: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

📱 App Permissions

This app only uses minimal permissions to display ads and does not request access to contacts, location, or camera.
